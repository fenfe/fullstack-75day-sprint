# 🛠️ Contractor MVP Calculation Blueprint

## 1. The Brick Formula Pattern
• A single-skin brick wall requires roughly 55 bricks per square meter.
• Formula: Total Bricks = (Wall Length * Wall Height) * 55

## 2. The Cement & Sand Pattern (The Mixing Matrix)
• For every 1000 bricks laid, a contractor needs roughly 3 bags of cement and 1 cubic meter of sand.
• Formula: Cement Bags = (Total Bricks / 1000) * 3
• Formula: Sand Volume = (Total Bricks / 1000) * 1
